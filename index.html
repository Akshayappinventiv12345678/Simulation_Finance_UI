Financial Simulation
Simulation Brief

This simulation helps apply the concepts learned during the program.

Structure: Decision-making on 24 market scenarios that CXO encounters in the course of an organization. Participants are encouraged to discuss the options presented and choose any option at the end of the scenario. The financial impact of the chosen option will be shown in updated financial statements after each scenario.

Duration: Each scenario is expected to take 10 minutes. One can move to next scenario or end the simulation to exit the exercise. Please download your final financial statements at the end of the simulation. Data might be available afterward. You might not be able to go back to the previous scenario or the previous simulation briefs.

Participant Brief

As a CXO of an Indian MSME manufacturing organization,

The Board of Directors and shareholders expect the organization to increase its top line by 30% year on Year and have a profit after tax of at least 20%.

According to market conditions, there are various opportunities/scenarios to increase sales, control costs, and improve cash flow. This can be through commercial bidding, investing in new products, expanding to new markets, investing in new capital equipment or M&A opportunities.

Decisions can be taken based on various scenarios presented continuously during simulation.
