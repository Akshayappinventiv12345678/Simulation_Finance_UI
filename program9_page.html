Marketing and Business Development for Growth

Program Overview

The Marketing and Business Development for Growth program is designed to help mid-career professionals and leaders develop and refine key strategies for growing their business. The program focuses on optimizing marketing efforts and business development tactics to capture new market opportunities and drive sustainable growth. Participants will explore:

  • Identifying and capitalizing on seasonal sales demand
  • Developing strategies to capture market share from competitors
  • Reactivating dormant sales channels

Through realistic business scenarios, participants will enhance their ability to analyze market trends, optimize marketing campaigns, and strengthen business development initiatives for long-term success.

Mini Case Study

Optimizing Marketing Strategies and Business Development for Growth

Market Situation:

GlobalFusion Industries, a major player in the motor vehicle aftermarket industry, has consistently experienced solid growth, supported by increased demand for sustainable products. However, the company is facing new challenges in maintaining its growth trajectory.

Competitors have started to offer similar products at lower prices, capturing a larger portion of the market. Additionally, several of GlobalFusion’s sales channels have become inactive, reducing their ability to reach key customer segments. To stay competitive, the company must reactivate these channels and effectively capture seasonal sales demand, while also implementing strategies to capture market share from competitors.

By focusing on these critical areas, GlobalFusion aims to enhance its marketing and business development efforts, drive customer engagement, and achieve sustainable growth.

Key Highlights

  • Seasonal Sales Strategies: Teaches participants how to identify and leverage seasonal demand to boost sales during peak periods.
  • Competitive Positioning: Equips participants with tools to capture market share from competitors through strategic pricing and marketing tactics.
  • Channel Activation: Focuses on reactivating dormant sales channels to expand market reach and boost overall business performance.
  • Real-World Simulations: Provides participants with hands-on experience through scenarios that simulate market dynamics and competitive challenges.
  • Sustainable Business Growth: Helps professionals develop long-term marketing and business development strategies for continued success.
