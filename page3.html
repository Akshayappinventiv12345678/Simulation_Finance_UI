Scenario 1: Bidding for New Projects
(Updated values post scenario 1)

Market Condition: The company identifies a major upcoming infrastructure project in its industry that requires industrial components similar to its offerings. Management sees this as an opportunity to secure a large, long-term contract, which could lead to consistent revenue streams over multiple years.

Options for Management:

  • 1. Prepare a Competitive Bid for the Project:
    • Pros: Potential for a long-term revenue stream.
    • Cons: High competition; bidding process is costly and time-consuming.
  • 2. Collaborate with Another Firm to Bid Jointly:
    • Pros: Shares risks and costs.
    • Cons: Must share profits if the bid is successful.

Investment Required:

1. Bid Preparation Costs INR 1,50,000

2. Administrative Expenses for Joint Bid INR 75,000

Output gained:

1. Revenue from Successful Bid INR 50,00,000

2. INR 25,00,000 Shared revenue in a joint bid collaboration
