Strategic Thinking and Change Management

Program Overview

The Strategic Thinking and Change Management program is designed to equip professionals with the skills necessary to drive strategic growth and navigate complex change management initiatives.

Core Program Focus

  • Strategic planning and execution
  • Change management and organizational development
  • Leadership and communication
  • Collaboration and stakeholder engagement

Key Learning Objectives

  1. Develop strategic thinking and planning skills to drive business growth.
  2. Analyze and navigate complex change management initiatives.
  3. Develop leadership and communication skills to effectively lead and manage change.
  4. Foster collaboration and stakeholder engagement to drive strategic growth.

Scenario Descriptions

  • Scenario 1: Strategic Partnership with a Non-Competing Manufacturer: Developing a strategic partnership with a non-competing manufacturer.
  • Scenario 2: International Competitor Entry: Responding to the entry of an international competitor in the market.
  • Scenario 3: Merger and Acquisition - Acquire a Smaller Competitor: Evaluating the strategic implications of acquiring a smaller competitor.
  • Scenario 4: Sales Demand Fluctuation Due to New Regulations: Responding to fluctuations in sales demand due to new regulations.
