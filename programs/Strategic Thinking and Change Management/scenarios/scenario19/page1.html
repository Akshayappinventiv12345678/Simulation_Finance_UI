Scenario 2: Merger and Acquisition - Acquire Smaller Competitor

Market Condition: The company has the opportunity to acquire a smaller competitor that has complementary products, a niche market share, and strategic assets. This acquisition could allow the company to expand its product range, customer base, and potentially increase revenue, but it requires significant capital investment.

Options for Management:

  • 1. Proceed with the Acquisition: Pay cash or finance the deal.
    • Pros: Gain access to new markets and increased economies of scale.
    • Cons: High financial risk and integration issues.
  • 2. Strategic Partnership: Instead of a full acquisition, pursue a strategic partnership or joint venture.
    • Pros: Benefit from the competitor’s expertise and market presence without the full financial burden.
    • Cons: May not gain full control over the assets and decisions.

Investment Required: INR 55 Lakhs for acquisition.

Output Gained:

  • Inventory of INR 20,00,000.
  • Property, Plant & Equipment of INR 30,00,000.
