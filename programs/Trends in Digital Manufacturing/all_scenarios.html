Trends in Digital Manufacturing

Program Overview

The Trends in Digital Manufacturing program is designed to equip professionals with the skills necessary to navigate the latest trends and technologies in digital manufacturing.

Core Program Focus

  • Digital manufacturing technologies and trends
  • Additive manufacturing (3D printing) and its applications
  • Digital twin technology and smart factory implementation
  • Industry 4.0 and the future of manufacturing

Key Learning Objectives

  1. Understand the latest trends and technologies in digital manufacturing.
  2. Analyze the applications and benefits of additive manufacturing (3D printing).
  3. Develop strategies for implementing digital twin technology and smart factory solutions.
  4. Evaluate the potential implications of Industry 4.0 on manufacturing operations.

Scenario Descriptions

  • Scenario 1: Sudden Decrease in Sales due to Market Competition: Responding to a sudden decrease in sales due to market competition.
  • Scenario 2: Responding to Requests for Proposals (RFP): Developing strategies for responding to requests for proposals (RFP) in the digital manufacturing industry.
  • Scenario 3: Implementing Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing): Evaluating the applications and benefits of implementing additive manufacturing (3D printing) in manufacturing operations.
  • Scenario 4: Developing a Smart Factory with Digital Twin Technology: Developing strategies for implementing digital twin technology and smart factory solutions in manufacturing operations.
