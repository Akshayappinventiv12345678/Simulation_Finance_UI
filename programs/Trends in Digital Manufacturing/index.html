Trends in Digital Manufacturing

Program Overview

The Trends in Digital Manufacturing Program is designed to equip mid-career professionals and leaders with the knowledge and strategies to navigate emerging digital trends in the manufacturing industry. This program focuses on:

  • Emerging trends: Additive manufacturing / 3D printing
  • The potential of smart factories and digital twins
  • Strategies to stay competitive in the evolving manufacturing landscape

Through a series of realistic simulation scenarios, participants will engage in strategic decision-making processes that drive innovation and maintain a competitive advantage for the fictional manufacturing organization GlobalFusion. Participants will navigate through incremental challenges that reflect real-world technological advancements and competitive dynamics in a rapidly evolving manufacturing environment.

Mini Case Study

Adapting to Emerging Digital Manufacturing Trends

Market Situation:

GlobalFusion Industries, a leading supplier in the motor vehicle aftermarket industry, has seen a steady increase in sales. Recently, the company has experienced steady growth, driven by increasing demand for sustainable manufacturing solutions.

To sustain and enhance this growth, GlobalFusion recognizes the need to integrate advanced digital manufacturing technologies, such as additive manufacturing (3D printing) and smart factory systems, into its operations. GlobalFusion must stay abreast of evolving trends in digital manufacturing to maintain its competitive edge and meet changing customer demands.
