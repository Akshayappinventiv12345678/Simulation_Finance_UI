Financial Analysis for Decision-Making

Program Overview

The Financial Analysis for Decision-Making program is designed to equip professionals with the skills necessary to make informed financial decisions.

Core Program Focus

  • Financial statement analysis
  • Ratio analysis
  • Cash flow analysis
  • Financial modeling

Key Learning Objectives

  1. Apply financial analysis techniques to inform business decisions.
  2. Interpret financial statements to assess organizational performance.
  3. Develop financial models to forecast future performance.
  4. Analyze cash flow to identify potential risks and opportunities.

Scenario Descriptions

  • Scenario 1: Participating in RFQ for New Bids: Financial analysis for RFQ participation.
  • Scenario 2: Optimize Pricing Strategy: Applying financial analysis to optimize pricing strategies.
  • Scenario 3: Cash Flow Crisis - Due to Delays in Customer Payments: Managing cash flow crises due to delayed customer payments.
  • Scenario 4: Cash Flow Crisis - Reduce Operational Costs: Reducing operational costs to manage cash flow crises.
