Financial Analysis for Decision-Making Program

Program Overview

The Financial Analysis for Decision-Making Program is designed to equip mid-career professionals and leaders with essential financial strategies for interpreting and analyzing financial statements, understanding key financial performance indicators, and applying financial analysis techniques. The program focuses on:

  • Interpreting financial statements (Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Cash Flow)
  • Applying financial analysis techniques (Ratio Analysis, Trend Analysis)
  • Understanding financial performance indicators

Through a series of realistic simulation scenarios, participants will enhance their ability to analyze financial health, make informed decisions, and drive sustainable growth for the manufacturing organization GlobalFusion Industries. The program reflects real-world complexities faced in dynamic financial environments.

Key Highlights

  • Financial Statement Analysis: Equips participants with skills to interpret key financial documents like the Balance Sheet, Income Statement, and Cash Flow Statement.

  • Cash Flow Management: Teaches strategies for managing cash flow crises, including responding to delayed payments and increased operational costs.
  • Pricing Strategy Optimization: Guides participants in making strategic pricing decisions, balancing cost-plus vs. value-based pricing models.
  • Real-World Scenarios: Provides hands-on learning experiences through real-world financial scenarios and simulations, such as bidding for new contracts and optimizing financial strategies during crises.
