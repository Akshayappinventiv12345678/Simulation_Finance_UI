Marketing and Business Development for Growth

Program Overview

The Marketing and Business Development for Growth program is designed to equip professionals with the skills necessary to drive business growth through effective marketing and business development strategies.

Core Program Focus

  • Market analysis and segmentation
  • Competitive analysis and strategy
  • Sales channel development and management
  • Digital marketing and social media strategy

Key Learning Objectives

  1. Develop effective marketing and business development strategies to drive business growth.
  2. Analyze market trends and competitor activity to inform business decisions.
  3. Design and implement sales channel development and management strategies.
  4. Create digital marketing and social media campaigns to reach target audiences.

Scenario Descriptions

  • Scenario 1: Capturing Seasonal Sales Demand: Developing a marketing strategy to capture seasonal sales demand.
  • Scenario 2: Capturing Market Share from Competitors: Developing a competitive strategy to capture market share from competitors.
  • Scenario 3: Reactivating Dormant Sales Channels: Developing a strategy to reactivate dormant sales channels.
