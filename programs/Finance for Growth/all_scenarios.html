Finance for Growth Program

Program Overview

The Finance for Growth program is a professional development initiative that equips business professionals with advanced financial management skills.

Core Program Focus

  • Strategic Financial Management
  • Business Development Strategies
  • Customer Engagement and Revenue Optimization
  • Financial Resource Allocation and Growth Initiatives

Key Learning Objectives

  1. Develop sophisticated financial strategies for business expansion.
  2. Master techniques for customer reactivation and relationship management.
  3. Create innovative strategies for sales team development and contract acquisition.
  4. Build resilience in managing financial challenges and cash flow complexities.

Scenario Descriptions

  • Scenario 1: Reaching Out to Old Inactive Customer: Financial approach to customer reactivation.
  • Scenario 2: Organizing Sales Day at Customer Location: Innovative customer engagement strategies from a financial perspective.
  • Scenario 3: Expanding the Sales Team for New Business Development: Financial considerations involved in organizational growth through sales team expansion.
  • Scenario 4: Bid for a New Contract: Financial complexities involved in competitive contract bidding.
  • Scenario 5: Cash Flow Crisis - Pursue External Financing: Financial crisis management and external financing strategies.
