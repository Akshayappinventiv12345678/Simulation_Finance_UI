Finance for Growth

Program Overview

The Finance for Growth Program is designed to equip mid-career professionals and leaders with the key financial strategies for scaling businesses. This program focuses on:

  • Resource allocation and cost estimation
  • Preparing budgets and monitoring progress
  • Financial forecasting techniques

Through realistic simulation scenarios, participants will engage in decision-making processes that drive sustainable growth for the fictional manufacturing company, GlobalFusion.

Mini Case Study

Strategic Bidding and Capital Investment for Expansion

Market Situation:

GlobalFusion Industries, a key supplier in the motor vehicle aftermarket industry, has been experiencing steady sales growth. This growth is largely driven by increased demand for sustainable manufacturing solutions.

To take advantage of new opportunities, GlobalFusion plans to bid for significant contracts and invest in advanced machinery to improve production capabilities. However, with current cash flow limitations, the company needs substantial capital investment to achieve these goals. GlobalFusion is considering investing in new machinery that promises higher output and lower operational costs.

Key Highlights

  • Financial Planning: Teaches resource allocation, cost estimation, and budgeting for business growth.
  • Strategic Investment: Covers strategies for making informed capital investments to boost production efficiency.
  • Cash Flow Management: Provides insights on managing cash flow and pursuing external financing options when needed.
  • Real-World Simulations: Engages participants in challenges like bidding for contracts and managing financial crises.
  • Sustainable Growth: Equips leaders with the tools to plan long-term financial strategies for scaling their businesses.
