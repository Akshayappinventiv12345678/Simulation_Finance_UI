Concepts:

Strategic Investment: Balancing high-impact technological investments with cost-effective optimization to maximize ROI and maintain operational stability.

Operational Efficiency: Leveraging advanced digital tools to enhance design accuracy, reduce prototyping costs, and accelerate product development cycles.

Risk Management: Proactively identifying and mitigating risks associated with technological integration and market competition to ensure smooth implementation.

Stakeholder Engagement: Involving key stakeholders in the evaluation process to gather diverse insights and secure buy-in for chosen strategic initiatives.

Investment vs Operational View: Distinguishing between investments in long-term assets and operational costs for accurate financial reporting and strategic planning.
