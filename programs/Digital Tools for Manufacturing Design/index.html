Digital Tools for Manufacturing Design

Program Overview

The Digital Tools for Manufacturing Design Program is designed to equip mid-career professionals and design engineers with the knowledge and tools to create designs that are easy to manufacture while meeting functional requirements. This program focuses on:

  • Application of CAD/CAM in manufacturing design
  • Role of simulation and modeling in product development
  • Principles of Design for Manufacturability (DFM)

Through a series of realistic simulation scenarios, participants will master CAD/CAM applications, learn how to integrate simulation, modeling, and Design for Manufacturability (DFM) principles into product development, resulting in more efficient and cost-effective manufacturing processes. Participants will navigate through incremental challenges that reflect real-world technological advancements and competitive dynamics in a rapidly evolving manufacturing environment.
